Vision

A Catalyst for AI Innovation

Pillars is a leading Saudi AI holding company headquartered in Riyadh, focused on advancing artificial intelligence (AI) technologies in Saudi Arabia and the broader MENA region. As a catalyst for innovation, Pillars is dedicated to building new ventures, forging strategic partnerships, and nurturing AI technologies through collaboration with international enterprises.

We are committed to driving AI innovation, enabling digital transformation, and enhancing the regional AI ecosystem by fostering high-impact projects and ventures in sectors ranging from security to talent development.
